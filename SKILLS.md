# Skills demonstrated in this repo

> *A human-readable index of the CNC and shop-floor methods this repository captures, with an emphasis on the parts of machining that matter before the spindle even starts.*

## CNC setup and fixturing

- **[haas-cnc-workholding](skills/haas-cnc-workholding.md)** *(canonical: this repo)* — design setups and fixtures around what the part wants to do when the cutter touches it, especially for awkward AM parts and one-off R&D work.

## Cross-repo links

- [`tensile-testing`](https://github.com/tonykoop/tensile-testing) uses this skill upstream when cylindrical blanks need to become standard test specimens.
- [`metal-powder-flow-device`](https://github.com/tonykoop/metal-powder-flow-device) shows the kind of hybrid AM + CNC part where setup logic matters as much as toolpath logic.
- [`additive-manufacturing`](https://github.com/tonykoop/additive-manufacturing) provides the upstream LPBF context for many of the parts that land on the Haas floor.
